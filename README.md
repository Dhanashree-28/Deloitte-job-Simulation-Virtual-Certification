# Deloitte-job-Simulation-Virtual-Certification
During the Deloitte Australia Data Analytics Job Simulation, I worked on a business case that involved analyzing and interpreting organizational data to support decision-making. I began by reviewing and validating the dataset in Excel, ensuring data quality and identifying relevant patterns and trends. Using analytical techniques, I examined key business metrics, classified information, and generated insights to address business challenges. I then created an interactive Tableau dashboard to visualize the findings through charts, KPIs, and trend analysis, making the information easier for stakeholders to understand. Finally, I translated the analytical findings into actionable business recommendations and communicated the results in a clear, data-driven manner, demonstrating the ability to combine technical analysis with business storytelling.
